- # Meeting discuss the concept of [[Deal Analyzer]]
	- ## What is [[Deal Analyzer]]
		- Deal Analyzer is a tool that captures non cancellable[[Multi Period Deals]]. Today this only happens for quotes via CRM. In the future it should capture everything from quotes, orders, ... In CRM there is a tab added that needs to be manually filled out when they want to register [[Multi Period Deals]]. Once the tab is completed it will be captured by [[Deal Analyzer]] and starts the approval workflow
			- Based on certain thresholds different levels of approval are needed.
				- Certain deals auto approve
				- Other deals need to be reviewed by credit, legal and even up to our Global CFO.
			- Every deal gets a Deal Analyzer ID for future references like yearly renewals for a certain approved contract.
